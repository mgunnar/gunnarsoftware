I am a software developer who excels in rapidly changing environments and collaborating within dynamic teams. Professional Summary & Education

With hands-on experience across the entire software lifecycle, I have been responsible for creating new features and fixing bugs in Java APIs for the financial sector. I have proven experience in migrating systems to a microservices architecture with Java 8 and 17, and in development with Node.js and React.js. My work includes creating unit, integration, and end-to-end tests, as well as optimizing scripts for Oracle and SQL Server databases.

I participated in the StartDB training program, where I enhanced my knowledge of Typescript and agile methodologies. My fluency in English was consolidated by working on an international team with members of various nationalities. My academic journey began with a Technical Course in Informatics (IFRS), evolved to a Bachelor's degree in Law, which sharpened my analytical and critical thinking skills, and culminated in a degree in Systems Analysis and Development, followed by postgraduate studies in Software Engineering and Software for the Aeronautical Industry at CIn/UFPE.
